About TravelEasy

Welcome to TravelEasy, your ultimate travel booking platform. Our journey began with a passion for making travel accessible and convenient for everyone. We envisioned a platform where users could effortlessly book flights, trains, and buses, all in one place.

This project is not just about meeting academic requirements; it's a manifestation of our commitment to simplifying the travel experience. As students of Holberton School, we've dedicated time and effort to create a solution that reflects our passion for technology and travel.

Throughout the development process, we've strived to provide a user-friendly interface, reliable booking services, and a seamless experience from start to finish. TravelEasy is more than a project; it's a testament to our dedication to innovation and problem-solving.

This project is part of our portfolio for Holberton School. You can find the source code on our GitHub repository.
